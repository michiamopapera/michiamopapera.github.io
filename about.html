The Camel contains over 6000 medical multiple choice questions collected over the course of nearly a decade by various students at Fed II, arranged into a simple and easily accessed site by me.

The Camel is a work in progress. The Plan:
  • March 2025... Tidying up question data, resolving questions with missing answers, addition of Head + Neck Questions
  • May 2025... Ability to sort by English or Italian language questions... OR Exam date information? not sure which to prioritise
  • Summer 2025... Improved database browser for easy viewing of questions.
  • ...and more one day. Image questions currently not supported which is a shame.
