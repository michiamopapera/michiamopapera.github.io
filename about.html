Welcome to Blue Camel!

Blue Camel contains over 8400 medical multiple choice questions collected over the course of nearly a decade by various students of Medicine and Surgery in English at the University of Naples 'Federico II'. All of the questions available here are from past exams, past ADE exams, with a few medical admissions questions thrown in as an extra.

Blue Camel has two simple goals:

  1. To be the simplest and easiest way to practise multiple choice exams at Federico II.
  2. To become the biggest completely free repository of medical multiple choice questions on the internet
...and we really mean free! No paying with your time or data either.
Blue Camel uses no cookies, no trackers, no ads, no paywalls. Just free information and learning with a simple static site, the way that the internet was intended to be used.
