The Camel contains over 6000 medical multiple choice questions collected over the course of nearly a decade by various students at Fed II, arranged into a simple and easily accessed site by me.

The Camel is a work in progress.

Future plans for the Camel include the following features:
>built-in database browser for easy viewing of questions.
>Instant feedback on each question after submitting answers.
>Ability to sort by English or Italian language questions.

>You can help by donating, or by verifying answers.
