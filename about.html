Blue Camel contains over 6000 medical multiple choice questions collected over the course of nearly a decade by various students at Fed II, arranged into a simple and easily accessed site by me.
The Camel is a work in progress.

Changelog:

  • 19.01.25 Support for Italian language questions added.
  • 25.01.25 Cardiology answers fully resolved, admissions Qs added.
  • 27.01.25 Tweaks to overall look and layout, better functionality for BC Answers.
  • 29.01.25 Mobile compatibility issues fixed. First official release!

Roadmap:
  • By April 2025... Tidy up question data, resolve questions with missing answers.
  • By Summer 2025... Add questions for Lab Med, Head and Neck, Neuro and Psych.
